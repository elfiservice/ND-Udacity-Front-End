The Kanguru

The Kangaroos living in Australia, and Only there! It's very comum to see some ones across the highways. Stay alert.

  • Scientific Name: Macropus
  • Average Length: Can be 2 meters
  • Average Lifespan: between six, 20 years varying by species
  • Habitat: Forests

The Australian government estimates that 34.3 million kangaroos lived within the commercial harvest areas of Australia in 2011, up from 25.1 million one year earlier.
